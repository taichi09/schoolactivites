Responsive Design

Responsive design in web development ensures websites adapt to different devices and screen sizes, employing flexible layouts, adaptable images, and media queries. By providing a consistent and user-friendly experience across desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones, responsive design eliminates the need for separate designs. It has become a standard practice, optimizing accessibility and usability in our mobile-centric digital landscape.
